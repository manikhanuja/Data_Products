Predicting Species on Iris Data Set

Classification Trees

Mani Khanuja
Data Scientist

Objective

  • Create a shiny app to perform predicitive analysis.
  • Identify the species based on Sepal & Petal characteristics
  • Algorithm used: Classification Trees
  • User enters the length & width of Petal & sepal and alogrithm predicts the species based on the Iris data set.
